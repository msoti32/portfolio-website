About Marko Sotirovic

I’m a UX/UI Designer who graduated from the Illinois Institute of Art with a Bachelor’s in Graphic Design. I came primarily from a print background and shortly after starting my first print-focused design role, I recognized early on that the future was in web and application design. I was a bit frustrated with my lack of education in this new realm and realized if I was going to ever make it, I had to do it on my own and going back to school wasn’t an option. So needless to say, after about 8 months in my first position I took a gamble amidst the economy crash and dove straight into the (very) deep end of web development and began a primarily self-taught adventure.

Taking a chance on myself proved to me and forever ingrained in me that you can do anything you set your mind to.

After learning how to swim in the world of front-end development and a short but highly educational period in the agency world, I knew it was time to go freelance full-time. During this time, I got to work on every medium imaginable and for some pretty amazing clients. I faced deadlines and challenges that really helped me grow as a designer and thinker. The word “No” didn’t exit my lips and I accepted any task that came my way.
